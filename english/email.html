Passengers (2016)

Passengers is a romantic science fiction film directed by Morten Tyldum. The film stars Chris Pratt as Jim Preston and Jennifer Lawrence as Aurora Lane. Passengers offers a visually stunning experience in a distant and dark universe. The story is full of setbacks and unexpected moments.

The plot is very unpredictable. The story takes place on the huge spaceship Avalon, which carries thousands of people. To the distant colony planet Homestead II, a journey that takes 120 years. However, due to a malfunction, Jim and Aurora wake up from hibernation 90 years early. What causes this fatal malfunction?

Jim Preston and Aurora Lane are interesting and well-developed characters. The actors bring them to life in a believable and engaging way. The script in the film is well written and matches the tone of the story. While not particularly funny, they convey the emotions and thoughts of the characters well.

The special effects in the film are impressive, especially in the scenes where the characters float in zero gravity. The sets and costumes are also well done, creating a believable and visually appealing world in which the characters live.

Overall, Passengers is an entertaining and memorable film that successfully combines elements of science fiction and romance. I recommend it to anyone who enjoys space-themed film. Who knows when it will happen to us?
